Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that aims to create machines capable of intelligent behavior.
AI can assist in research by organizing study materials, summarizing articles, and generating insights.
There are several types of AI, including supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning.
Ethical AI focuses on preventing bias, maintaining privacy, and ensuring that AI outputs are safe and reliable.
Students and researchers can benefit from AI tools to accelerate learning and discovery without compromising academic integrity.
